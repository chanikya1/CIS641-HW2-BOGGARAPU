What is Cricket?

Cricket is a popular team sport played with a bat and ball, involving two teams of eleven players each. The game is played on a field with a pitch at its center, where the batting team aims to score runs while the bowling team tries to dismiss the players. Cricket has different formats, including Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) games, making it a versatile and globally loved sport.

Who is Virat Kohli?

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time. Born on November 5, 1988, Kohli made his debut for the Indian national team in 2008. Known for his aggressive batting style, remarkable consistency, and strong leadership, Kohli has broken numerous records across all formats of the game. He was the captain of the Indian team in both Tests and limited-overs formats and is known for his passion and fitness on the field. Kohli's determination and hard work have made him an inspiration to many young cricketers worldwide.

Virat Kohli's International Records

  • Test Cricket Highest Score: 254* vs South Africa in 2019
  • ODI Cricket Highest Score: 183 vs Pakistan in 2012
  • T20 International Highest Score: 122* vs Afghanistan in 2022
